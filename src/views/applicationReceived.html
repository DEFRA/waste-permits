What happens next

We’ll check the documents you’ve uploaded, your technical qualification and any site location. We’ll also run checks on the permit holder and any other relevant people.

It takes up to 13 weeks in total to issue a permit. It may take longer if the information you gave us does not meet our requirements.

We won’t issue a permit if we believe that your operation will pollute the environment or harm human health.
