A organisation or group does not usually have the legal status needed to apply for and hold a permit.

If the permit is for many properties we recommend that you apply as a management company.

If the club, association or other group has post holders such as Chair, Treasurer and Secretary, they should all apply for the permit.

If fewer than 6 people run the group, we recommend that all those running the group should apply.

Warning The people named in the application will hold the permit in their own right. They will be personally responsible for the operation of the permit.
