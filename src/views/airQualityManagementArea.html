Is any plant or generator in an Air Quality Management Area (AQMA) or if mobile, do you want the option of deploying to an AQMA?
How to check if the site is in an AQMA
  1. Go to the AQMAs interactive map on the Defra UK AIR website (opens new tab).
  2. Select the ‘AQMA Boundaries’ option to display the AQMAs.
  3. Search for a postcode or find the area on the map and zoom in to check if you are in an AQMA.
  4. Select the AQMA on the map to read the AQMA name.

Find out the background level of nitrogen dioxide
  1. Go to the annual and exceedance statistics on the Defra UK AIR website (opens new tab).
  2. Select the local authority for the site from the Sub Zone list.
  3. Select ‘Annual Mean’ from the statistic list.
