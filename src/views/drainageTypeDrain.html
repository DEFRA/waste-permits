The area where you store undamaged vehicles before dismantling must prevent water running off and causing pollution.

The surface must be impermeable. This means it must not allow liquids through. For example, it could be tarmac, concrete or asphalt, with no cracks.

And it must use a sealed drainage system or an oil separator. This means that the only acceptable drainage for the surface is to:

  • drain into a sewer, but only if the water company gives you a formal consent
  • collect in a sump for disposal by tanker
  • use an oil separator (interceptor)
