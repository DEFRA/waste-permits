More information about water use
Water use for site operations can include but is not limited to:
  • dust suppression, odour control systems and water to wash down bays or equipment
  • fire prevention measures including the storage and use of water to extinguish fires
  • use of water as part of your manufacturing processes, cooling systems or steam generation
  • use of water to aid waste treatment activities
