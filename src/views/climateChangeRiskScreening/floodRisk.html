What is your site’s risk of flooding from rivers or the sea?
How to identify your site's flood risk
  1. Open the ‘Learn more about flood risk’ map (opens new tab)
  2. Select 'Basic view' and 'Flood risk from rivers or the sea'
  3. Zoom in to the maximum magnification that includes your whole site
  4. Click within the boundary of your site to find your flood risk level
