You’ll need to ask us to do a habitat assessment if your operation’s screening distance is within:

  • 2km of a designated site of special scientific interest (SSSI) for any fuel
  • 5km of a designated special protection area (SPA), special area of conservation (SAC) or Ramsar site if the fuel is natural gas or low sulphur diesel
  • 10km of a designated SPA, SAC or Ramsar site if the fuel is biogas or landfill gas

If you’re not sure if there’s a relevant habitat within the screening distance of your site, then you can order a free nature and heritage conservation check (opens new tab) or check using the Defra MAGIC mapping website (opens a new tab).
