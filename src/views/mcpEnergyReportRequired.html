Is your MCP or SG new or substantially refurbished?
How to decide if it is new or substantially refurbished
Plant is considered to be new if it is:
  • put into operation on or after 20 December 2018
  • altered or repaired such that the emission limit values (ELV) change - for example if an existing boiler is running on heavy oil and it is converted to run on natural gas
  • substantially refurbished, meaning that the cost of refurbishment was more than 50% of the investment cost for a new comparable MCP unit
